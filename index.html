Earthquakes in USA

Because the United States is at the plate boundary, earthquakes often occur in the United States. According to the records of the U.S. Geological Survey, in the past 10 years, more than 700 earthquakes of magnitude 4 or above occurred in the United States.

The potential damage of earthqaukes needs consider both magnitude and depth.
